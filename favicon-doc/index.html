What is Iconcity?

Iconcity is a unique product responsible for generating favicons. Favicons are what allows your webpages to stand out from other web pages. It can be noticed as an icon visible next in the title of tabs, a bookmark icon and many others.

What distinguishes us from others?

ICONCITY will provide a user friendly, and responsive website with robust documentation and community where designers and developers can collaborate and provide clients with a unique user experience
